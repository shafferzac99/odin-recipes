Zac's Signature Oatmeal

Description

I have this as often as I can. Usually for breakfast but if I'm in need of a quick meal this is hard to beat

Ingredients

    1 cup oatmeal
  • 1 3/4 cup water
  • 2 tbsp chunky peanut butter
  • 1 tbsp honey
  • 1 tsp cinnamon
  • handful of raisins
  • 1 banana

Instructions

  1. Follow instructions on oatmeal box. If you have a rice cooker, add oatmeal, water, and set to steam
  2. While oats cook, slice the banana
  3. Scoop cooked oats into a bowl
  4. Lay banana slices onto oats, followed by raisins, peanut butter, cinnamon, and finally honey.
  5. Enjoy!
