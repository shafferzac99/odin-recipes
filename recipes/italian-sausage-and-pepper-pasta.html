Sausage and Pepper Pasta

Description

This has been a go-to recipe for me as a college runner. It is carb-heavy while still having protein and vegetables, and is really easy to make

Ingredients

  • 2 lb Italian Sausage (about one large link)
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 red or yellow bell pepper
  • 1 yellow onion
  • 1 24oz jar of pasta sauce
  • 1/2 tsp dried basil
  • 1/2 tsp dried oregano
  • 1/4 crushed red pepper
  • 1/2 lb penne pasta
  • Kosher salt

Instructions

  1. Heat a large skillet on medium-high heat. Add oil and sausage. Brown sausage on each side for a few minutes. It does not need to be cooked through.
  2. Remove sausage to a cutting board and slice into medallions. Return sausage to skillet and cook the sausage through, until browned on cut sides. Remove sausage
  3. Slice bell pepper and onion into thin strips. Add to skillet, stir until they soften slightly.
  4. Add sausage back to skillet with pasta sauce, basil, oregano, and red pepper. Stir to combine.
  5. Bring pot of water to a boil, salting generously. Follow instructions on box, then drain the pasta, saving a bit of pasta water. Add pasta back to pot, then pour pasta water and sauce on top and combine.
  6. Enjoy!
