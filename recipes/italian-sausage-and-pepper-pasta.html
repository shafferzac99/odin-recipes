Sausage and Pepper Pasta

This has been a go-to recipe for me as a college runner. It is carb-heavy while still having protein and vegetables, and is really easy to make

Ingredients

  • 2 lb Italian Sausage (about one large link)
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 red or yellow bell pepper
  • 1 yellow onion
  • 1 24oz jar of pasta sauce
  • 1/2 tsp dried basil
  • 1/2 tsp dried oregano
  • 1/4 crushed red pepper
  • 1/2 lb penne pasta
